How To Trade

To carry out financial trading via CFD you need to register on our website. Initially the user has "wood" grade, and can only trade in "cryptocurrencies" category markets. By progressing (accumulating experience points), the other categories will be unlocked. The trading page looks like a table of markets, filtered by a category, selectable through a list. The columns of the table are as follows: icon, title, description, buy, sell. The "buy" and "sell" fields are composed of numerical sales and purchase values of the single market. When a user decides to buy a market at the indicated price, it's added to his personal list of markets, and can decide to sell it at the indicated value at any time (these values are constantly changing).

Wood Rank

You can unlock this rank registering to our website, the privileges are the following:

  • An initial capital of 1000€ of credits on your user wallet;
  • Access only to Cryptocurrencies category.

Bronze Rank

You can unlock this rank by reaching 100 points experience, the privileges are the following:

  • A credits bonus of 500€ on your personal wallet;
  • Access to Cryptocurrencies and Currencies categories.

Silver Rank

You can unlock this rank by reaching 300 points experience, the privileges are the following:

  • A credits bonus of 1000€ on your personal wallet;
  • Access to Cryptocurrencies, Currencies and Commodities categories.

Gold Rank

You can unlock this rank by reaching 500 points experience, the privileges are the following:

  • A credits bonus of 2000€ on your personal wallet;
  • Access to Cryptocurrencies, Currencies, Commodities, Indices and Stocks categories.
