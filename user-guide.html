User Guide

This is a financial trading platform by CFD (contract for difference), in the "Home CFD" section you can view and buy the quotes of the markets at the real time price.
After you bought a quote of a market, you can check your open transaction (with the relative purchase price) and sell it in the "Wallet" section.
The profit is defined by the difference between the price sell and price buy: Profit = Price Sell - Price Buy.
Doing actions you can unlock Goals, you can check them in the "Goals" section and see the condition for unlock them and the experience that you can gain.
Reaching a certain amount of experience, you can upgrade your rank: 0 EXP for Wood (initial rank), 100 EXP for Bronze, 300 EXP for Silver, 500 EXP for Gold.
With different ranks you can view different markets categories:
Wood only Cryptocurrencies, Bronze only Cryptocurrencies and Currencies, Silver only Cryptocurrencies, Currencies and Commodities,
Gold all categories (Cryptocurrencies, Currencies, Commodities, Stocks and Indices).
Everytime you reach a new rank, you can receive a random packet as award of different types:
Common (50 credits), Rare (100 credits), Ultra Rare (200 credits). You can check and open your packets in the "Packets" section.
In the "History" section you can find all the closed transactions with the relative profit, at the footer you can find the sum of your purchases, sellings and the relative total profit.
In the "Deposit Credits" you can deposit credits by using PayPal, Credit Card or Bank Transfer. After the deposit your wallet will be upgraded immediatly.
